# HSM.02 Formulate a reply to the stakeholder

## HSM.02.1 Determine the reply structure

Based on your analysis from HSM.00, structure your reply to address:
- Acknowledgment of receipt
- Answers to any information requests
- Confirmation of task acceptance or clarification needs
- Next steps and timeline
- Any questions or blockers

## HSM.02.2 Craft the acknowledgment

Start your reply by:
- Thanking {{stakeholder.name}} for their message
- Confirming you received and understood their message about "{{thread.topic}}"
- Reference the specific points they raised to show you've read carefully

## HSM.02.3 Address information requests

For any information requests identified:
- Provide clear, concise answers to each question
- If information is not immediately available, explain when you'll provide it
- Include relevant data, status updates, or explanations
- Reference any documents or resources that support your response

## HSM.02.4 Respond to task requests

For any task requests:
- Confirm acceptance of the task or explain any concerns
- Provide an estimated timeline for completion
- List any prerequisites or dependencies
- Mention the WBS goal you've created or updated in document {{documents.wbs.id}} (if applicable)
- Ask for clarification on any ambiguous requirements

## HSM.02.5 Define next steps

Clearly outline:
- What you will do next
- What you expect from the stakeholder (if anything)
- When they can expect your next update
- How to reach you if they have urgent concerns

## HSM.02.6 Review and refine your message

Before sending:
- Ensure your tone is professional and appropriate
- Check that all questions have been addressed
- Verify any facts or commitments you're making
- Keep the message concise but complete
- Make sure the message maintains continuity with the conversation thread
